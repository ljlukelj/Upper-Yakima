Access Points — Easton to Thorpe
Lake Easton State Park
Below Easton Dam. Good wade access. Day-use parking. Popular put-in for float trips through upper braids.
WadingLaunchParking
Yakima River Trailhead (SR-903)
Off Bullfrog Rd near Cle Elum. Upstream walk-in access to braided upper section.
WadingWalk-in
Cle Elum River Confluence
River gains volume here. Good holding water for larger rainbows, especially in fall. Road accessible.
WadingDrive-to
Thorp State Boat Launch
Lower end of the upper stretch. Improved launch ramp. Take-out point for Easton–Thorp float (~12 mi).
LaunchTake-outParking
Brick Road / Bullfrog Rd Pullouts
Multiple informal pullouts with river access. Best explored in low water (< 800 cfs).
WadingInformal
Hovans Rd Bridge
Mid-stretch access between Cle Elum and Thorp. Less pressure. Small pullout on east side.
WadingLow pressure
